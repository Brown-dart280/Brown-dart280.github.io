The Black Blade

By. Maurice Brown

Many has fallen to my blade

Challengers from afar which to test my might

None have been worthy

I crave a challenge, only a true swordsman can give

Yet my name seems to spark a sense of courage in the un trained

Who will be be the first to cut down the black blade?

A of these unworthy have dulled my blade, my sense

The killing becomes easy to my now, my sword stikes divine

For, I alone

Am unrivaled under the heavens
